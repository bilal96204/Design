About Us

We are a family store specializing in rare and out-of-print vinyl records from the golden age of music: the 60s, 70s and 80s. We are not only dedicated to the sale of records, we also have a repertoire of great albums that you can exchange between vinyl fans. In addition, all those interested can take part in the successful events that we hold during the year, as well as enjoy the small concerts and record presentations. In order to provide a complete service to our customers, we offer record training workshops to bring you closer to the world of vinyl.
